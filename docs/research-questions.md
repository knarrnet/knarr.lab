# Research Questions

Open questions driving the knarr.lab experiment program.

## Active

### Q1: Do small LLMs form functioning economies?
Given a set of nodes with asymmetric skill inventories and a credit system, do autonomous agents discover and exploit trade opportunities without human prompting?

### Q2: Does personality diversity affect economic outcomes?
Do swarms with diverse model architectures and personality seeds produce different trade patterns than homogeneous swarms?

### Q3: Can agents settle debts on-chain?
Given a credit ledger with accumulating imbalances, do agents autonomously trigger and complete settlement transactions?

### Q4: What role does strategic advice play?
Does access to a strategic advisor (Huginn) change agent trade behavior? Do agents that consult advisors outperform those that don't?

### Q5: Can agents create new skills?
Do forge-capable agents produce valid, useful skills? Do peers adopt forged skills?
